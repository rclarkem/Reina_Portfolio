About Me

Hi there! I'm Reina Mitchell. I am a full stack developer with experience in languages such as Ruby, JavaScript, Golang, and Python, however, some of my favorite projects involve working with front end frameworks like React for web development.

With a background in public policy, wanting to make the world a better place for others has always been my passion. It is this passion that has greatly influenced my style as a programmer. I hope to build more applications that make political engagement and policy issues such as healthcare more accessible to users.

When I am not coding, I love to travel and explore new foods and cultures. I also enjoy photography, rock climbing, and spending time with my dog.
